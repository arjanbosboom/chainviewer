# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Demonstrating empathy and respect
- Giving and accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the project
- Showing courtesy toward other community members

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment or discrimination
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Public or private intimidation
- Publishing others' private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior and may take appropriate action in response to unacceptable behavior.

## Reporting

Report unacceptable behavior to the project maintainers through the repository contact channels once the repository is published.
